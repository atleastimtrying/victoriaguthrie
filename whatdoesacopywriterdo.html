What Does a Copywriter Do?

This is a question many people ask, along with ‘Can a copywriter bring me more customers?’ and ‘Why should I hire a copywriter?’ which we’ll go into in other blog posts. Here is my breakdown of exactly what a copywriter does in less than 250 words.

cop•y•writ•er [kop-ee-rahy-ter] noun

a person employed to write advertising copy

Who are they?

  • An expert in writing for different audiences
  • Skilled in writing persuasive copy that can lead to sales
  • Able to write in many different tones of voice and styles
  • Highly trained (usually with a degree in some form of writing)
  • Skilled at planning and researching
  • 100% focussed on fulfilling the target

What do they do?

  • Write text for website pages, blog posts, social media, marketing – in fact, just about anything
  • Sculpt compelling, original content to provide your audience with valuable information
  • Make sure that the copy they’ve written fulfills its intended purpose
  • Persuade your audience
  • Ask lots of questions
  • Bring more people to your business
  • Help to give you more conversions
  • Engage your audience
  • Work with the you to give you exactly what you need and want
  • Help to translate and clarify your ideas about your business

Who do they work with?

  • Small businesses
  • Sole traders
  • Creative agencies
  • Large corporate businesses
